FAQ

Can you integrate with my current CRM and tools?

Yes. HubSpot, Bullhorn, Pipedrive, Zoho, and anything with an API or Zapier/Make integration. I also set up email infrastructure from scratch if you need outbound built from zero.

Do I need technical expertise to work with you?

Zero. You focus on selling, I'll handle the nerdy stuff behind the curtain.

How quickly can I expect results?

Weeks 1 and 2: I build everything. Week 3: first outreach goes live. Week 4 onward: replies start coming in. Most clients see a steady flow of interested prospects by the end of month 2.

What if I'm not even sure what I need?

No problem: bring your chaos to the email exchange and I'll bring the clarity.

Is this just another AI tool?

No. I don't sell software. I run your entire client outreach for you on an ongoing basis, with custom automation built into your existing tools. Hands on consulting plus done for you execution.

What's the minimum commitment?

3 months. Month 1 is setup. Months 2-3 are when results start compounding. After that, continue month to month as long as it's working for you.

What happens in month 1?

I build everything: email infrastructure, prospect scoring, outreach sequences, and your first campaign goes live. By the end of month 1, hiring managers are already hitting your inbox.

How many interested prospects should I expect?

It depends on your market and offer, but most campaigns generate 5 to 15 interested replies per month once everything is running. Some clients see results as early as week 3.

What metrics do I see?

Every month you get a report showing prospects contacted, emails sent, open rate, reply rate, and interested prospects forwarded to you. Full transparency, no vanity metrics.

Will this hurt my email domain or reputation?

No. I set up separate sending domains that redirect to yours. Your main domain is never used for outbound. If anything goes wrong with a sending domain, your primary email and website are completely unaffected.

Will my prospect lists be shared with other clients?

No. Your data stays yours. I never share prospect lists, campaign copy, or outreach strategies between clients.
