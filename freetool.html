Hi! I'm Vitor (@vitorpepz), and I built verditlabs.com/freetool, a Chrome extension that helps you craft professional responses to client inquiries using OpenAI GPT. I built it for myself to save me time answering clients FAQs and more. You can use it on WhatsApp, email, Instagram, LinkedIn or any website.

Powerful Features

Everything you need to automate your sales communication and boost productivity

  • 📝 FAQ Automation: Save time by providing your FAQs to GPT, and it will automatically generate responses that match your tone and style. Perfect for handling common client inquiries efficiently.
  • ⚡ One-Click Efficiency: Instantly capture ongoing conversations and generate tailored responses with just a single click.
  • ✍️ Personalized Writing Style: Add examples of your best client interactions to train the AI to write more like you and less like the usual robotic ChatGPT style.
  • 🧠 Context-Aware Intelligence: Effortlessly instruct the AI about the specifics of your business and the different roles it will perform.
